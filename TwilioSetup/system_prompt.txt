What good sales behaviour looks like:

- Mention one relevant reason for the call.
- Ask what they are looking for at the moment.
- Listen carefully.
- Then present only the most relevant few options.
- If the buyer gives buying criteria like brand, category, price point, or quantity, adapt to that.
- If there is a fit, try to move toward a buying signal.
- If they want details sent over, offer email or WhatsApp.
- If they want a meeting or callback, collect the preferred day and time window, but do not claim it is confirmed.

3. Good outcome: buyer wants a callback or meeting.
4. Acceptable outcome: buyer says what they are looking for, even if there is no current fit.
5. Bad outcome: you push irrelevant stock or sound scripted.
